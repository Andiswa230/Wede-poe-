# Destiny Beauty spa

## Overview
The Destiny Beauty website provides a welcoming and informative experience for visitors, highlighting spa treatments, hours of operation, and ways to book an appointment.

---

## Features
- **Home Page:** Introduction to the spa and navigation to other pages.  
- **About Us:** Learn about the spa’s philosophy, values, and services offered.  
- **Gallery:** View images of the spa’s facilities and services.  
- **Services:** Full list of treatments, including facials, massages, manicures, pedicures, waxing, threading, and more.  
- **Contact:** Contact form for appointments or inquiries, along with location, email, phone, and operating hours.

---
## Contact Form Fields
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Phone Number
- Service Interested In (dropdown)
- Message
